The Artist

ARi
Hotep

Artist / Producer Barber / Entrepreneur Creative Director

ARi Hotep is an independent hip-hop artist from Memphis, Tennessee blending Southern-rooted storytelling, soulful street perspective, and entrepreneurial ambition into a sound that bridges generations of hip-hop culture. Influenced by the authenticity of Outkast, UGK, Devin the Dude, Wu-Tang, Rakim, Snoop Dogg, DJ Quick, Curren$y, and Dungeon Family-era creativity, his music reflects real-life hustle, reflection, and elevated Southern lifestyle. His single "Parking Lot Pimpin'" continues building regional radio support across multiple U.S. markets.
